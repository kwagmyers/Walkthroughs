Introduction

This walkthrough is based on Narodnaya Solyanka Release 2018. If you're looking for a specific task, see the Tasks by Character section.

Tips∨
  • Many items, such as artifact, cigarettes, and mutant parts, are needed to complete some tasks. It's recommended to only sell ammo and, if you have more than one, weapons and suits. See Items to Keep.
  • The Bar/Rostok is a central location in the first part of the game and a great place to store extra supplies. Once you get to Pripyat, consider moving your cache to Radar.
  • Missions from various NPCs often involve the same location and I've done my best to gang them up together.
  • Vodka makes you hungry and thirsty, so only use it for radiation when absolutely necessary.
  • Most food makes you thirsty, with the exception of Tourist's Delight and mushrooms. It's best to wait and eat when the thirsty icon shows (or the hunger icon turns red) to make the most of your water supply.
  • Beer helps thirst, but not as well as water. And never drink before going into battle.
  • Artifacts have a limited lifespan. I'm not sure if it's just while hanging on your belt or not. So, if you don't need it, put it in a backpack.
  • All corpses are eventually removed, so don't use them for stashes unless it's for a very short time.
  • Anomalies move when you leave a map and return. If you're cooking an artifact, you have to stay on the map until the cooking process completes.

Start∨

You start the game near the crash of the death truck in Cordon with a Loner standing nearby named Astrologer. Talk to him and he sends you to the Northern farm to collect a stash, and then on to the Rookie village. Your ammo is limited, so avoid mutants for now. Use the pistol to open the stash, and head south toward the railroad embankment. You can bribe the military, as Astrologer suggests, or go up the embankment west of the military to the fence and follow the fence east to the opening at the bridge. The anomaly doesn't harm you, just messes your vision up for a few seconds. Go west on the tracks and jump onto the shipping container between the box car and fence. This gets you over the fence so you can continue on the the Rookie village. You can also go southeast and cross the tracks where the fence ends. But you'll have to deal with Bandits, Military, and radiation. If you do bribe one of the privates, I've read that you can do some work for the major. But the only time I tried, he either wouldn't talk or was dead. Doc and Sidorovich are at the west end, just outside Sidorovich's bunker. Talk to Doc to get a dose of medicine that knocks you out. When you wake up, you're in the Cave.

Cave∨

You spawn with a teleport and campfire one way and an intersecting passage the other. You can see on the minimap that there is a neutral NPC just around the corner. Go talk to him (note that the passage closes behind you) and he takes you to Miner, who gives you the Bring the container to Miner task. You have some nice weaponry, but you can do this without firing a shot. In fact, you may want to drop some weight and remove any artifacts that reduce your energy.

  1. Take the first two lefts back the way you came in.
  2. Turn right into a large room (again, the passage closes behind you) and stay along the right wall. At this point, if you haven't started sprinting, do it now.
  3. Turn right again and follow the passage down an incline and past a campfire.
  4. Continue down the passage and left at the hanging sheer-black vegetation. Remember this spot as a transition to Garbage opens later in the game.
  5. At the campfire, turn right to see an Electro anomaly. Find the ladder and climb down (even though it doesn't go to the top). Use the nearest and furthest campfire to help align with the ladder and avoid a fall.
  6. Sprint-jump over the right or left campfire and follow the passage to the container. Note that Miner told you not to kill the Fleshes, but they will attack.
  7. Picking up the container spawns a Bloodsucker, but you can sprint back to the ladder before it gets to you. Don't stop until you're up the ladder again.
  8. Follow your path back to the large room and go to the far-right corner.
  9. Turn left and follow the passage back to Miner, who then gives you some more medicine (in your backpack), sending you back to the Cordon.
Work for Sidorovich∨

You wake up in one of the Rookie Village root cellars. Feel free to snatch the Playboy for an easy 169 RUs. Sidorovich is now in his bunker. Go talk to him and get the Get the information from the scout task of the vanilla SoC. On the way, be sure to help Tolik and get the Find five dog tails task from him. Also, search the soldier inside the trailer to start the Find the military PDA task. Kostya is a new character that spawns in the Rookie village once you save Nimble and turn over the flash drive to Sidorovich. He stands in the fence opening on the south side of the road at the west end. Talk to him to get the Find the cache in the Cordon task. Next, talk to Wolf and get a Jellyfish artifact, which you can use if you got the Find the Jellyfish artifact task from Nimble. Note that Nimble's tasks are optional. There are other optional tasks you can easily complete to help build your inventory:

  • Ask Wolf what you can do to help and he gives you the Bring the document to Wolf task. The reward is an AKS-74U. This document is behind a mattress in the shipping container as you make your way through the tunnel with the moving Electro anomaly.
  • Accept the Find the Night Star artifact task from Sidorovich, and sprint to get the artifact before another stalker beats you to it. The easiest way to find the artifact is to go through the tunnel under the road near where you found the injured Tolik. Continue southeast in a straight line and you should be able to see it not far from the tunnel in the valley south of where you first met Petruha. If it's not there, keep checking the area as you move around the Cordon. Night Stars are fairly common and you're sure to find one eventually. The reward is an eight-artifact container to store radioactive artifacts.
  • Two tasks from the vanilla version are also available from Sidorovich: Destroy the bandits in the Agroprom underground and Kill the bandit leader. You have to do these two things anyway, so you might as well get something for it.
  • Find a jacket for Nimble is a matter of opening the manhole cover (Shift+F) on the south side of the train hanger near the crane, climbing down to get the jacket out of a backpack (that can be used as a stash box), and taking it to him. Be sure to take it with you when you go to Lab X-18 as you'll end up in Cordon and can save yourself an extra trip.

From the Rookie village, make your way to the bus stop and find the Goldfish artifact that you'll need for another task. This artifact gives off radiation, so put it in the container you got from Sidorovich. Next, search the Mill for more supplies. Here you'll find Doc and Astrologer, but they don't have anything to offer at the moment. There are two fuel cans that you can sell to other stalkers (traders won't buy them).

Strelok's Hideout∨

Meet up with Fox and follow the vanilla storyline to Bes, Seriy, Mole, and Strelok's hideout. If you save Yurik from the Bandits when you first entered Garbage, you can pick up the gun on your way to Agroprom. Just be sure to have it with you when you head for Lab X-18. I suggest that you wait to find Kostya's Find the cache in the Garbage until you come back from Agroprom. Also, while in Garbage you may get a message from a wounded stalker asking for help. Or, this may come when you return. This is really a Bandit ambush, and there may be another Bandit hidden in the Flea Market. Take them both out and when you approach the Bandit calling for help, you get a text message about Mercenaries in the area. They spawn on the hill east of the Bandit. You can sprint back to the Flea Market and use the rubble for cover. The Bandit calling for help has the Wounded Bandit's PDA, which is added to your PDA's Diary under Search for the missing scientists and the Find the military PDA task is updated to show that you now need to find the Bandit (Friar) who ripped off this Bandit. This is what I love about Bandits, they don't care about each other at all. You kill one and another comes to loot the dead, never checking to see what, or who, killed them.

If you take Fox's task for medkits, he only accepts the red ones. He asks about Army Medkits, but even if you have them he only takes the red ones. Also, whenever you enter the Agroprom Underground, it's a good idea to check both ends of the traveling Electro anomaly for artifacts.

In Strelok's hideout, find the floor grid that you can pick up (Shift+F), move it out of the way, and follow the passage into the next room. Loot the backpack on the bed and search the small safe up on top of the large electrical box. Find your way out of the underground and steal the case in the army base. I've tried to do this stealthily, but it's easier just to sprint to the third floor. At some point you get a message from Gray, a trader who likes to drop in from time to time. If you're like me, you have a backpack full of weapons to sell, so his arrival is always welcomed. Also, if you offered to help Mole find his PDA, now is a good time to go into the train tunnel and fetch it.

Bar/Rostok tasks∨

Help the Duty outpost with the mutants, Find the cache in the Garbage, and talk to Prapor about Fox's weapon. See Prapor tasks for additional information. Ignore the message from a wounded stalker for now. As you approach the gate you get an SMS from Baldy at the Bar. Continue on to the Bar, but be wary as you past the Duty checkpoint as there is an Avenger waiting to take you out. In Rostok, there is another Avenger lurking behind the small building on the right past the southern Duty checkpoint. In the Bar, talk to Baldy and get the Search for the treasure task. This opens transitions from Garbage and Agroprom to Cave, where you met Miner. Other people to talk to are:

  • Find the family rifle - This is now in Darkscape and covered here.
  • Help Brom - Also in Darkscape and covered here.
  • Bring the Desert Eagle to Freeman - This is in Wild Territory and covered here.
  • Bring the unusual artifact to Plichko - This is part of Search for the treasure.

Search for the treasure∨

Accept this task from Baldy at the Bar to open transitions to the Cave. This procedure starts at the Garbage train tunnel.

  1. Go to the back of the tunnel to transition to the Cave. You spawn near the hanging sheer-black vegetation mentioned in Cave.
  2. Go straight (west) and follow the passage left and up the incline.
  3. Take the first two lefts into the large room.
  4. Go to the far corner and turn left.
  5. Follow this passage, staying to the right, to Miner.

Ask Miner for the Monolith fragment you left with him (on your first trip to the Cave) and he sends you to the Doctor. Miner also asks you to pick up a book in the Forgotten Forest. This is an easy task and presents the opportunity for more tasks. But first, we need to get Baldy's treasure and Plichko's artifact.

  1. Follow the steps in Cave to the point where you climb down the ladder.
  2. Go right and right again. There are numerous mutants here, including up to four bloodsuckers. So, start throwing grenades. Killing a flesh doesn't seem to harm your reputation with Miner.
  3. Once the way is clear, follow the passage east, then south to the three stalagmites surrounded by Heat anomalies.
  4. Break the clay jar to find Baldy's coins. NOTE: There have been occasions where the jar wasn't visible and I had to walk around the bones (without stepping into an anomaly) until I actually kicked it. Then it appeared.
  5. Now back to the end of the passage (and campfire) and turn right (north).
  6. Follow the passage until you get to an intersection with two campfires.
  7. Turn right at the first campfire and continue south until you see another ladder.
  8. Take the ladder up and notice the small hole to the left. As you approach, some rats spawn. Take them out quickly, or they'll do the same to you.
  9. Go through the hole and work your way through the anomalies until you come to the Emerald artifact (sometimes there are two).
  10. In the large room, take the passage on the right. When you encounter an Electro anomaly block your path, throw a bolt into it and sprint through as it discharges.
  11. Continue to an opening to the right where a small stash box may have something for you. This passage takes you back to the large room.
  12. Take the passage in the southeast corner and turn left at the next intersection.
  13. Follow this passage and turn right at the campfire to go back to the Garbage transition.

You can return to Baldy and Plichko if you want, or go directly to Dark Valley. The Garbage to Forgotten Forest transition isn't available yet to Get the book for Miner. This transition opens when you return Fox's gun.

Lab X-18∨

Again, be sure you have Fox's gun, Yurik's gun, and Nimble's jacket. Head for the Dark Valley Bandit Base to get Borov's key, and follow the vanilla storyline until you clear out the X-18 factory complex. Note that Borov has a document, "Borov's Notes" that mentions Rat. This task does not show on your PDA, but is addressed here. Before entering the lab, put all artifacts in one of the stash boxes. I recommend the stash box in the trailer near the northeast corner of the factory. When you enter the lab, all of your artifacts are taken by the security system. Don't plan on using the corpse just inside the lab as a stash, as it won't be there when you get back. If you do have an extra backpack, it's a good idea to take it with you into the lab to store artifacts that you find there and can use on your multiple return trips.

But first, you have to clear out the factory complex. As you do, you may notice a neutral character in the south building. This is Messer and, if you kill his guards and give him a med kit, he gives you information on a Snotty Porcupine. You may also find the Bandit who stole the Military PDA. If not, he'll show up eventually. Once you find him, refer to Get 10 scientific kits for Prapor. With the complex cleared of Bandits, you now have to deal with the electrified bloodsucker guarding the lab door. My strategy is to have a grenade ready as I walk down the steps toward the lab. Then I throw the grenade as soon as I hear the bloodsucker spawn (about halfway down the first hallway), and sprint back up the stairs while pulling out the shotgun. Most of the time, the grenade does the job, but you may need to finish the bloodsucker off with the shotgun. Just don't let it get too close. It's like a running Electro anomaly.

Check the dead stalker for the note to Kolya. The only real difference in the lab are the mutants and a Notepad with entries on the first dead scientist. Also, break the mirror in the lavatory next to the first dead scientist to get the Military Note. This is not listed on your PDA Tasks, but in Diary under Personal notes through searching > Search for Kalugin. On the second level of the lab, in the small room off the room with the second dead scientist, the refrigerator has 20 cans of beer. If you took the Get beer for Bes, he only wants five cans. You can sell the rest, or save them in case you run out of water.

From the bottom of the second-level stairs, take the left hallway to the end and turn right into a room with lockers and a document on top. This is a Simbion artifact recipe that you may need later. Pick it up and find it on the PDA's Encyclopedia page under Recipes.

Now kill the Pyrogeist and grab the document. When you exit the lab, don't plan on using the front gate as there is a BTR waiting. Instead, go to the northeast corner and use the boxes to jump to the pipe on the north wall, and then out the window. Now west across the road and either around or through the swamp. Cross the road again and stay low as there are Bandits in the pig farm. As you approach the gate to Darkscape, another BTR approaches with a support squad. Use a tree as cover and start throwing grenades. Two or three grenades is all you need to take out the BTR.

Help Brom∨

When you spawn in Darkscape, there's a white Lada waiting for you just up the road on the left. If you are not familiar with operating zone vehicles, the controls are listed in main Options with the exception of braking, which is the space bar. Also, you may need to assign the Start Engine control in the General section. Also, if the Lada gets on its side, turn the wheel toward the ground and go forward to get back on all four tires. For a bonus stash, stop on the bridge going over the creek and walk toward the pumping station on the left (north). Just to the right of the pumping station is a cave with a dead Bandit and a backpack with goodies.

At some point your PDA quits on you and you must go to the Bar and have the Associate Professor fix it. You must give Garik a candy bar to get past him, and then step back so he can move away from the door.

The Bandits are camped in a fuel station located in the southeast corner of the map. If you approach from the west, you may be able to get into the building and out again without firing a shot. Find the main hallway going west and take the last doorway on the right. Go all the way to the last office and find the safe. Notice that the door is open just enough so that you can lean left to see the case on the top shelf. Grab it and take the road west until you come to a tree partially blocking the road. On the other side of the tree are some stalkers, including Bes, Mole, and possibly Fox. Give Bes his beer and ask if there is another job. He gives you the Bring Bes the box of ammo task. The ammo is on the tail section of the helicopter on the north side of the Vehicle Graveyard. Give Mole his PDA. If Fox is not at the campfire with Mole, check your PDA to see if he's on his way. You may be able to meet him on the road. When you give Fox his gun, the Garbage to Forgotten Forest opens and you can Get the book for Miner when we get back to Garbage.

Find the family rifle∨

To retrieve Hunter's family rifle, there is a mining shaft that opens directly north of Bes' camp. You can also get there from the west side of the broken train bridge, but it's a long walk. Coming from the south, follow the passage to a large opening with civilian zombies. To the right is a passage leading to the rifle, which is guarded by four armed zombies and a controller. Find the rifle in the center of the area on a table. Be sure to collect as many zombie hands as you can. You will soon be getting a request for 10.

Check your PDA for Yurik and take him the modified MP5. Ask if he has any more work, and he asks you for a Burer hand (which you probably have from X-18). When you're ready to leave Darkscape, follow the road west into the tunnel and transport back to the Cordon. Sidorovich greets you with the usual, "...stop by and we'll chat." only it's in Russian. Wait to take the Steal the box from the outpost task. Give Nimble his jacket and head back to the Garbage. At this point in the game, you can either leave the Lada at the Military Outpost, or go back to Darkscape, Dark Valley, and then to Garbage. This might be a good time to grab Akim's document and Bes' ammo. Since you're close to Akim, take the document to him and get the Bring old documents to Akim, pt. 2 task.

Back to Agroprom∨

There are a couple of items to take care of before moving on. We need to get Akim's second document and open the Rostok to ATP transition. Go back to the factory where you first met Mole and introduce yourself to Adrenaline, who is a trader on the second floor of the southeast building. Ask if he knows of any paths and he gives you Bring the disc to Adrenaline. But first, head for the underground to get Akim's document (see Bring old documents to Akim, pt. 2). Take the document back to Akim and get the next task, Bring old documents to Akim, pt. 3.

From Akim, head back to the Bar. Along the way, check the two large tanks near the Flea Market. If there is a corpse in the eastern tank, climb the ladder on the west side and enter the tank using the opening. This is the scientist mentioned in the previous dead scientist's note. This one has the Scientist's Notepad with an entry about parts to a door code. See ATP, Radar, and for locations of the code.

Lab X-16∨

Back to the Bar and talk with Garik about Petrenko's thermoses. Next, talk to Lanky about Petrenko's flask. Lanky asks for five Wrenched artifacts, 50,000 RUs, and 10 microcircuits (which we'll get later). Now follow the vanilla storyline to save Kruglov and progress to Yantar. Be sure to grab Wolfhound's PDA. He is usually in the small building on the south end of the large tanks near the crashed helicopter. Just before you get to the Yantar transition you get an SOS from a stalker. When you spawn in Yantar, two Bandits are standing over the stalker's body. Take them out and get the Note from a dead stalker. This prompts a message to Barman, who then responds, promising to call the stalker's family.

Sakharov sells a bioradar for 330,000 RUs. That's a high price, but well worth it as it shows enemies before you see them.

Ask Sakharov if he has any work for you and he offers the Find Ghost's PDA task. Then, help Kruglov with the measurements. My strategy for this task is to use a shotgun with slugs or darts, and to go ahead of Kruglov and clear out all the zombies in the area. Kruglov stops at the tunnel under the road and waits for you to join him before taking his first measurements. Next, retrieve Vasiliev's information and proceed to Lab X-16. Once you find Ghost and put the controller down, be sure to Find Ghost's PDA. Turn in the suit and PDA to Sakharov and ask about transitions to get the Find the "Elder Glassbeads" artifact for Sakharov task, and then talk to Kruglov to get the Bring Ghost's Diary to the Scientist task. Next, take the X-16 documents to Barman. This opens the Rostok to Army Warehouses transition, our next destination. But before you leave, ask Barman about Ghost and he tells you about Ghost looking for his double. But before he gives you any more information, he requires a Simbion artifact. The recipe is in your PDA's Encyclopedia page.

Army Warehouses∨

Follow the storyline to Cook (not getting involved with Skull's group), and ask him how his drinking allows him to get anything done. He gives you the Bring the crow eggs to the Cook task that you can complete the next time you visit the Forgotten Forest. Next, talk to Skinflint and get the Clear the bloodsuckers from the village task. Then, talk to Lukash about Skull's group. When you complete the task, head for the village to kill Bloodsuckers and get Voronin's RG-6. It's a good idea to take the weapon back to Voronin before returning to the Freedom base to collect rewards. If the condition of the RG-6 is too bad he will reject it. See Take the RG-6 from the Freedom base on what to do if that happens. Ask Voronin if there are any other tasks and he gives you the Find the documents task. Ask about Fang to get the Bring the camouflaged exoskeleton task. This gives use three tasks to complete in ATP: Voronin's exo, Adrenaline's disc, and finding one part of the door code.

Before you leave Voronin, take a good hard look at the Boar's head on the wall, focusing on the mouth. In it is the Commander's Note. This is what the Military Note was referring to, i.e., the note in the broken mirror.

ATP∨

The Rostok to ATP transition is just west of the Rostok to Army Warehouses transition. See Bring the disc to Adrenaline for what to do next. Afterward, go northwest toward the spot where the road ends at a pond. From there, go north to find the group of stalkers and talk to Kalinin to get the Kill Shnyra task, which spawns an attack by the Bandits. You can also ask about transitions. For a Mama's Beads artifact, he tells you about ATP to Garbage at the east end of the road. After repelling the Bandit attack, go on the offensive and attack the base. Generally, the best cover is where the road enters on either side (east or west).

If you're a looter like me, your backpack is probably max'ed out. Attacking the Bandit base from the east offers a lot of cover and Gray likes to land near that location. If you drop a backpack near where the road enters the complex, you may be able to sell most of your loot.

With the base clear, see Bring the camouflaged exoskeleton for instructions on that task. If you took the Kill Shnyra task, you can go back for your reward and the next Kalinin task, or wait until the next trip to ATP. For the flash drive with the partial door code, follow this procedure:

  1. Locate the building with the Monolith sign and go north.
  2. Find the tall utility box with the number on the top panel (north side) matching the third entry of the Scientist's Notepad, PK10. Only it's in Russian and has a hyphen, so it looks more like nK-10. This flash drive contains the middle three numbers to the door code.
  3. Jump onto the fence post and move to north as far as possible without falling off.
  4. Sprint-jump onto the utility box.
  5. Search the cache and grab the flash drive.
Stealing Army Documents∨

With the camouflaged exoskeleton, you can easily complete Sidorovich's Steal the box from the outpost. After that, you can turn it in to Voronin (if you want). You will get your own later in the game. If you have any outstanding tasks with the guys in Darkscape, such as Yurik's Burer hand, Bes' ammo, or Fox's medkits, pack 'em up and head for Cordon by way of Dark Valley and Darkscape. Even though the camouflaged exoskeleton makes you really hard to see, BTRs have no trouble seeing you at a distance. Soldiers can only see you if they are really close. They know you're there, and point their weapons at you, but don't shoot. So, you have to approach from the east using the fallen tree along the east fence and jump over the fence. You don't need to land on the outhouse roof, because inside the outhouse are the microcircuits for Lanky. Now go north to the front door of the barracks and inside to get the case. When you leave, use the guard tower stairs in the southwest corner and jump over the south fence to avoid the BTR guarding the front gate. If the BTR is still outside the front gate, park you Lada near the south-side fence and use it to jump over. See Bring old documents to Akim, pt. 3 for instructions on getting those documents. If you haven't found Friar and the Military PDA yet, check the bodies by the door (this is usually when and where I find it). To exit the complex, go to the southwest corner and use the brick building's platform to jump on the top of the fence, and then to the ground (to avoid too much damage).

Sakharov Documents∨

Take the Dark Valley to Garbage transition, then to the Cordon transition to give Akim his documents. Tell him you have returned with the documents and he gives you the Bring Old Documents, 4 Suits and a PDA to Akim: Part 4 task. Also, ask him about the crow eggs and get the Bring the medicine to Akim task. Since you still have the camouflaged exoskeleton, now's a good time to do it. Back to the Bar to turn in Petrenko's Gauss pistol, Military Outpost box to Barman, and we can now talk to Kostya again to get the next cache. Petrenko offers the Exchange artifact task. As you leave Petrenko, find Captain Ivantsov among the group around the campfire. He gives you the Find the Playboy and Give the RG6 grenade launcher. You can also turn in the camouflaged exoskeleton suit and documents to Voronin. Now to Agroprom to give Adrenaline his disc and pick up Sakharov's documents, which are in the trailer in the middle of the swamp. When you give Adrenaline the disc, you get a task, Speak to Zahar, who is in Rostok.

He gives you the Find the cache in Laboratory X-18 task.

In X-18, the order in which you find Kostya's cache and Sakharov's document depends on the location of Kostya's hint.

Back to the Bar∨

Take the X-18 PDA back to Kostya and he gives you the next cache, Find the cache in the Agroprom. Drop off the suits to Petrenko. You may be tempted to keep one of the suits for the Akim task. But Petrenko won't take just one. Hopefully, you have acquired a Groza and have spoken to Guide about meeting Doc and can do that while in Agroprom. If not, there are other task options:

  • If you have Cross' artifacts, now might be a good time to complete that and Cook's eggs.
  • If you have the items for Fima Coal, you can complete the Box and Safe task.

Ghost and Fang∨

Picking up where you turn in the camouflaged exoskeleton to Voronin and he tells you to speak with the informant (Snitch) at the Bar, Snitch gives the Bring Tyrant's PDA to the informant task in the Swamp. If you have the Simbion artifact, give it to Barman to learn about Ghost wanting a look-alike. Barman sends you to speak with the scientists in Yantar. Go to Yantar and ask Kruglov if he made any progress on the diary. He gives you the Get the computer for the scientist task. However, Arhara is not available until we do some work in the Swamp, even though he shows on your map. If you go there, you won't find him.

At this point in the game you probably only have one transition to the Swamp, which is from Cordon. This is convenient since this transition is nearest the target. Also, vodka, cigarettes, and mushrooms are trading commodities for optional tasks in the Swamp. If you've been stashing them, this is a good time to put them in your backpack. You can trade red bloodsucker tentacles for an IMI UZI with Sviblov, too.

You may have one or more tasks requiring items that you may have not found in your travels. It's a good idea to keep an eye on your PDA task list and refer to the Tasks by Character section when you have the required items. Mostly, these are stand-alone tasks, meaning you can do them at any time without impacting your progress.
Rat∨

Borov's note mentions a character named Rat who made off with Borov's PDA, and is hiding in the Swamp. There is a possible bug here; if you go to the Clear Sky base before contacting Rat, he might get killed. This happened to me. So, I'm putting this series of tasks at the very start of entering the Swamp.

In Swamp, find Krysyk "Rat" camped directly south of the Agroprom transition. Talk to him to get the Fulfill the request of Krysyk task. This sends you back to the Bar to ask Barman if Rat can hide there. Barman wants Rat's cache, so you have to go back to the Swamp and give Rat Barman's terms. He agrees and gives you the coordinates of his stash, which is in Dark Valley, in the swamp west of the Bandit base. Get the artifacts from the cache and take them to Barman. Then, go back to Rat and get the location of Borov's PDA. Rat starts the long walk to the Bar. Don't be surprise if you encounter him on the road somewhere in between.

Clear Sky∨

The Swamp/Cordon transition is near a group from Clear Sky. Talk to Dyak, the leader of the group, and agree to lead his men in an attack on the Bandits. This is the Free the "Clear Sky" member from captivity task. Remember to get the PDA before a Clear Sky member loots the dead Tyrant. Clear the church, talk to the captive Clear Sky member, and report back to Dyak. Go south past the Fishing Hamlet and, if you like, take on some optional tasks from Cold and Vasily. But, you must take the first two of Sviblov's tasks. Refer to the Tasks by Character section for instructions.

During Sviblov's second task, Obtain a rifle for Sviblov, you meet Dan, who gives you the task that unlocks the Agroprom to Swamp transition and spawns Arhara. Go north from the Machine Yard and follow the fence west to the Agroprom transition. Meet Arhara and attack the institute building from the south through the hole in the fence, and then through a window. Go up to the third floor and find the dead scientist. Grab the parcel, head back to the Swamp, and give the parcel to Dan. Dan tells you to go back to Arhara, but finish up here in the Swamp first. Instead, go meet Cat, whose location is marked on your map. After completing the Search the corpse of a Monolith and find a map for Cat task, Cat goes to the Clear Sky base. Go with him and collect your reward from Cold. Sviblov has one more optional task for you if you like. Complete the Find the remaining Monoliths, Cat needs their personal notebooks task and head back to Agroprom.

Borov's PDA∨

See Arhara for a simple task in the Agroprom Underground and grab the cache from Rat with Borov's PDA. Give the notebook to Arhara, ask for the next task, and head to X-18. In X-18, grab a computer out of the control room where you found the documents for Barman on your first trip. Next, find the caches in Borov's PDA:

  1. Use the drain pipe on the west to enter the base compound and go straight (east), watching the platform above for Bandits.
  2. Follow the basin around to the right and use the pipes to jump up to the ledge along the western side.
  3. Follow the ledge south, then east, and then north to the code-locked door.
  4. Enter the access code from the PDA and start breaking boxes.
  5. Stand on the stacked firewood to find the backpack hidden in the overhead duct.
  6. Open the gate and break a few more boxes.
  7. When you are done collecting loot, exit the same way you came in and go to the Fuel Station.
  8. Search the pump with the same characters as in the PDA.

Now take the X-18 computer to Kruglov, who is able to decipher "Yakut... at... Warehouses". This does not on your list of tasks.

Meet La Havre∨

You should have a fairly long list of tasks in the Army Warehouses at this point:

  • Destroy the bloodsuckers in the village - Report back to Skinflint that the job is done.
  • Kill all of the Duty members at the farm - Likewise, you (probably) need to report back to Lukash that the job is done.
  • Bring the crow eggs to the Cook - And again, just report to Cook.
  • Get the sniper rifle from the Mercs - You get the rifle as part of another task yet to come.
  • Give the RG6 grenade launcher - Stop me if you've heard this, but give it to Skinflint.
  • Find the cache in the Army Warehouses - See Kostya for information.

You may also have Speak to Zahar, which you can do on your way from Yantar to Army Warehouses. Or, wait until you talk to Wolf and do both tasks at once. If you completed the Get the computer for the scientist, you need to speak with Yakut (he won't talk to you complete the Bring the documents from X-10 to Le Havre and speak with Freeman). If your task list differs, you can always reference the Tasks by Character section. There's is going to be some back-and-forth between the Bar and Army Warehouses. So, this is the time that I like to move my cache to the trailer next to Wolf. Eventually, I'll move it to Radar as that becomes the center of activity later on.

In Army Warehouses, finish up all your tasks and ask Lukash for more work to get the Follow the informant task. This will help you with Voronin's Get the sniper rifle from the Mercs task and you can collect Voronin's small thermoses. You can also talk to Max about a flash drive he wants. Then go to the southern barracks building along the tracks to meet La Havre and take the first two items on his list of tasks.

On the hill east of the village, find Wolf and ask why he's in Army Warehouses to get the Find and bring the military radio to Wolf task. This is also where you find Kostya (relocated from Rostok) and Yakut. But all Yakut can talk about is the Brain Scorcher, so you have to come back to him later.

Radar∨

Complete the first part of Protect the barrier and move on to Radar. You need to return to Army Warehouses so don't waste your time with reporting back to Lukash just yet. As you fight your way up the road to X-10, keep in mind the location of Kostya's Radar cache. For all but the train location, you may want to report back to Kostya with the PDA and save yourself a trip to the Monolith-occupied complex. Also, take a little side trip (north) up the road on the east side of the map. Check the bed of the truck to see if there is a document that opens the Radar to Dark Valley transition.

For Arhara's laptop, I recommend approaching the helicopter from the north as there are less mutants. Outside the complex wall, find the dead scientist and trailer. Inside the trailer is a document that opens the Yantar to Radar transition.

In X-10, Monolith is there to greet you. You should have the following tasks in X-10:

  • Flash drive with part of door code - In the room with platforms on the east and west side, a tank on each platform with three pipes along the floor pointing toward the center of the room. The western platform has a small electrical box on the south side. Search the front left edge of the box to find the flash drive with the first two numbers of the door code.
  • Collect scientific books
  • Bring the documents from X-10 to Le Havre

Turn the Brain Scorcher off. After the blackout, exit the lab and be sure to use the hole at the east end of the wall to trigger Sidorovich's message about meeting the Loners in Pripyat (otherwise, you may set up a bug). At this point, you can either follow the vanilla storyline and continue on to Pripyat, or complete the following tasks:

  • Collect scientific books - Use the Radar to Yantar transition in the northwest corner of the map to give Sakharov his documents.
  • Bring the laptop to Arhara's brother - Return to Army Warehouses and give Yakut the laptop.
  • Find the cache in the Radar - Return to Army Warehouses and give Kostya the PDA. He tells you the numbers from the PDAs in random order and gives you the Find and inspect the room behind the secret door in laboratory X-10 task.
  • Bring the documents from X-10 to Le Havre

I prefer to complete the storyline to get it out of the way, and to earn the cache that Lukash gives you for the Controller brains; a camouflaged exoskeleton suit of your very own.

Pripyat∨

Meet the Loners in Pripyat and get to the hotel. In the hotel, get the decoder and turn left (north) out of the room and down to the end of the hall. The last room on the right contains Voronin's large thermoses. Before you leave the hotel, be sure to take out as many Monolith as you can from the safety of the hotel. Especially the RPGer west of the Ferris Wheel as he overlooks a spawn point and can make it difficult when you need that spot clear for landing. Also, if you have a backpack full of loot to sell, the hotel is a good place to drop a cache backpack. A trader will be setting up shop nearby in the very near future.

Monolith Control∨

Go to the northeast corner of the room with the locked door and turn around to find a brown stash box containing the code for the door. Move around the equipment to the south and locate the keypad.

CNPP2∨

When you first step out of the doorway, take a look around for chimeras. Also, there's a sniper to the right. Go north around the wall and west to the break in the wall to trigger the three Monoliths. Before moving to the rooftops, continue north to the road and then east to find Lukash's Controller brains. If you had the Associate Professor enhance your PDA for Controllers, you shouldn't have too much trouble finding them. Just watch out for snipers. Also, grab any Gold Chunk artifacts that you'll need for later. About the time you get to the road going east and west, you get an SMS from Voronin. You can't do much about it until you complete the CNPP2 map. Once you have the Controller brains and Gold Chunks, go back to the one of the portals near the Monolith Control Center exit.

When you finish the CNPP2 gauntlet, you are teleported back to the Pripyat Ferris Wheel. Take La Havre the document and Lukash his Controller brains. Ask Lukash for the next task (See Lukash for instructions). See Kostya and get the Find and inspect the room behind the secret door in laboratory X-10 task. You still can't speak to Yakut about Ghost, but you can give him the laptop. Then, go see Freeman, take Voronin his thermoses, and he gives you the Help the Dutiers in Eastern Pripyat task. You can also ask for his help to get the FInd the computer and the video transmitter task.

Meet Bland∨

You get this task from Freeman as part of the Bring the documents from X-10 to Le Havre task. You also have the Find the computer and the video transmitter task that also takes you back to Dark Valley. It doesn't matter which you do first. Click the link to see Voronin's task instructions.

To meet Bland, you must clear the X-18 complex of Bandits and Mercs. I find it easiest to use the Lada to jump over the wall near the back and work my way to the front. When the last one falls, you get an SMS from Bland telling you he will meet and three neutral characters appear on your minimap. Go into the back building and talk to Bland. Give him the 50,000 RUs and take the flash drive to Freeman. The information on the flash drive implicates La Harve, but no task is listed in your PDA. When you confront La Harve, be sure to stash everything or you will lose it. When you wake, there is a PDA on the floor nearby. Pick it up to see that you need to take the PDA to the Assistant Professor at the Bar. You/Strelok also have a thought to talk with Yakut.

Yakut tells you about La Havre's group heading to the northwest checkpoint. The leader of the group (not La Havre) is marked on your map. Kill him and get the metal box he's carrying. Take that to Yakut and ask him about Ghost. He sends you back to the Agroprom Underground. Along the way, stop at the Bar and give Bolt's PDA to the Assistant Professor. If you have a Kolobok artifact, take it with you.

The Assistant Professor agrees to check the PDA, but wants a Kolobok artifact in return and promises to send you an SMS when the PDA is ready. This takes about 45 game-time minutes and you can either wait or come back later. You may notice that the speaking with the Assistant Professor and the artifact are two separate tasks in your PDA. You can get the PDA information without the artifact, but you still have it as an incomplete task. The information you get is, at the moment, of little use.

In the Agroprom Underground, as you near the ventilation shaft going to Strelok's hideout, you get a message from an unknown sender and the Find Ghost task. The message tells you to go to the Radar forest.

Find Ghost∨

From Agroprom, go Garbage > Dark Valley to get to Radar. In Radar, continue west through the hole in the fence and along the north side of the boulders. Follow the boulders around to find Ghost's camp on the south side. Talk to Ghost and get the Kill Tusk and get his flashdrive task. This task takes you to the northeast corner of the Swamp. Hopefully, you have a Night Star artifact and the dried bear paw for Kalmyk to make Cat's medicine. Drop the items off to Kalmyk and meet up with Owl.

With Tusk's group eliminated and Cat's medicine ready, return to the Bar. Cat has more information on Monolith and gives you the Get information of the Monolith in the Agroprom for Cat task. Now give Ghost the flash drive and he gives you the Kill Tusk. Part 2. Bring the documents to Ghost task in Cordon. For this task, I recommend bringing an RPG. From Radar, go to Yantar and drop off Sakharov's three books, then to Agroprom. Again, see the respective Tasks by Character entries for task instructions.

You may encounter a bug here where the freed Loners pick up weapons from the fallen Monolith and attack the Military in the institute complex. There doesn't seem to be any other option than to kill everything in the area so that you can talk to the Loners. If that happens, you can grab Lukash's dynamite on the second floor.

When you talk to the Loners, they ask you to guide them to the factory complex. Once they are safe, talk to them again to find out more, then move on to the Cordon and the Rookie Village. Complete the Tusk task and return to the Bar. Talk to Cat, who sends you to Skinflint on the Freedom base to find Rabinovich. If you have Lukash's dynamite, you can drop it off.

Find Rabinovich∨

Get the Get the ammo box from Sidorovich task from Skinflint. Ask Skinflint if there is anything exciting happening lately, and then ask him for help finding the trader Rabi. Skinflint sends you to the Bloodsucker Village (there's no update on your task list). Go to the farmstead on the southeast edge of the village and talk to Joker. Now go to the northwest edge to find a wounded Modest. Give him a medkit and he tells you he saw Rabi on the road to Pripyat. This spawns an ambush by Mercs to the north and east. Fight them off and head for Pripyat.

Stop by and give Ghost the Tusk document, then continue on to Pripyat. In Pripyat, go through the underground car park and north up the road on the east side of the hotel. There's heavy fighting between Monolith and everyone else, plus Duty and Freedom may go at it, too. Don't get too distracted by it until you meet with Rabinovich. Just before you get to the Ferris Wheel, you can see a neutral stalker in the building on the east side. Use the rubble to climb up and into the open window. In the next room is Rabinovich. Talk to him until he gives you the information you're after, then he collapses. Eventually, he becomes hostile and you have to put him down. You get a task update to return to Cat, and a new task Find the Black Doctor.

Hopefully, you encounter a Monolith named Charon. Put him down and loot his corpse to get the CacheCharon task.

Now for the fighting in the streets: Make your way west to the north side of Monolith Headquarters, i.e., the Palace of Culture. Enter from the north, turn right and then left. Take the stairs on the other side of the room. Go either way where the stairs turn and find Doc's book. Then go back upstairs to the large room with six Monolith in a trance. Take them out and notice on your minimap that two friendlies move in from the west. This is Doc (with his pseudodog) and Syak, a trader. Talk to Doc and give him his book. He gives you the Find the Doctor's Medallion task. Talk to Syak to get the Bring the toolbox to Sid task. Go back to the Bar and report to Cat. He sends you to Zahar, who gives you the Find Brother Louis in the Swamps task. Go Garbage > Agroprom > Swamp. Complete the first part of Find Brother Louis in the Swamps and use the nearby transition to Cordon.

Talk to Sidorovich:

  • Hey Sid, looks like you've had another hard day of sitting there drinking booze all day long. - This gets you the ammo for Skinflint.
  • Hey, Sid, you look like shit! You need to lay off the vodka for awhile. - This gets you the Metal box for Syak.

Be sure to have a Goldfish artifact with you and deliver Skinflint's ammo and get the Bring the information to Skinflint from the Deserter task. If you don't have a Taurus Raging Bull yet, buy one from Skinflint. Next, drop off Syak's toolbox. Get the Bring the Goldfish artifact and then the Bring the Briefcase in X-18 tasks. Then, move on to Help the Dutiers in Eastern Pripyat.

Eastern Pripyat∨

For this trip, I recommend a sniper rifle, short-range weapon for clearing a building, and the auto-firing Gauss you got from Ghost (save your ammo for the underground). Also, put as many of the following items in your backpack as you have:

  • Desert Eagle
  • Taurus Raging Bull
  • Goldfish artifact
  • TOZ-32
  • Uzi
  • Two Ravens Eye
  • AEK-971

You spawn in Eastern Pripyat near Plichko's group. Stash all but your sniper and assault rifles, then go talk to Plichko. Only talk to him about the Sports Complex, do not talk or ask about anything else or you set up a bug that prevents you from completing the task. See Help the Dutiers in Eastern Pripyat for instructions.

With the Sports Complex under Duty control, head northeast around the school to the Vine Anomaly. Matvei Kulinar is trapped by some Chimeras. If you have a Gauss gun, you can take out the Chimeras with ease. Matvei is up on the top floor of the north building. Approach the vine from the west, turn left and left again. When you get to the place where you can cross to another vine, turn right and continue up to Matvei's location. Ask him how you can help and he gives you the Help Matvei Kulinar task.

Complete the task and, as you leave the laundry mat, you meet someone coming the other way. This is Hector, Matvei's partner and repair technician. Then you get a message from Sakharov about some missing scientists and get the Find the scientist in Jupiter task. This opens transitions between Eastern Pripyat, Jupiter Underground, and Jupiter. Jupiter Underground is full of mutant spiders. This is why I suggested you bring the Gauss. Grab your stashed items and fill as many of Matvei's orders as you can. Use the stash box on the first floor (southeast corner) for incomplete orders and move on to Jupiter.

Jupiter∨

Check your map for the Jupiter Underground entrance, which is south of the laundry mat. Use a knife or crowbar to hit the door open. If that doesn't work, go to the other side and shoot the door from the back. Climb down the ladder, turn around and go south toward the door that appears to be blocked. You spawn to the underground, and now the door is west of the ladder (and when you return you must climb two ladders). Anyway, find the dead stalker and read his PDA. Then move on to encounter some snorks. When you get to the large room, it is filled with mutant spiders. Sprint to the ladder on the left (northwest) and up to the platform. From here you can safely snipe some of the spiders with the Gauss. But be conservative with your ammo as there are more spiders to come. Search the giant spiders for a Spider's Paw, which you will need later on. The smaller spiders can kill from a few feet away, even if you're not high enough off the ground. When the large room is clear, there is a bonus cache that probably belonged to the dead stalker:

  1. Go to the north end and find the cement slabs stacked up next to a large tank. Use the slabs to get on top of the tank.
  2. Go up the two small pipes and jump over the fence.
  3. Go up the ladder to the top without going over the rail.
  4. Go to the southwest edge of the platform and jump to the pipe to get to the next platform.
  5. Continue on around using the ledge to get to the next platform.
  6. Up the next ladder.
  7. Turn east and go across the two pipes.
  8. Use the electrical box to jump on the two pipes going down to the top of the overhead crane.
  9. Go to the motor section and cross (south).
  10. Turn east and up the two pipes.
  11. And again to the next platform.
  12. Continue on to find the room blocked by debris.
  13. In the cabinet with the microscope on top, look inside through the narrow opening to find the backpack with the loot.

Continue on through the underground and stay on top of vehicles and boxes as much as possible to avoid the small spiders. The underground passage ends at the large freight elevator, which is the transition to Jupiter.

In Jupiter, make your way out of the factory while watching for Mercs. As you continue through the administration building, you get an SMS from a wounded stalker and his location is marked on your map. Take the east road north to the stalker (Dimon Kalter), give him a medkit, and ask him what he is doing. He gives you the Help the stalker task. Follow Dimon around the north side of the quarry. When you get to the work camp, a pack of pseudodogs attack. You best option is, as you near the camp, stay below (south of) the top ridge (out of sight of the pseudodogs), sprint ahead, jump up on the truck and into the back. Keep moving around so the pseudodogs can't jump at you. With the way clear, take out your sniper rifle and continue following the stalker.

When you attack the train station, do not use explosives. This turns the scientists and trader against you.

Once the Bandits are eliminated, talk to Professor Plotnikov and agree to lead them to the mobile lab. Then go back to Dimon and report that there is a spy. This prompts the scientists to move out of the basement and out the south door (notice the working Lada). When you get them to the lab, talk to Plotnikov again and he tells you of a cache. You can also do some work for him. See Plotnikov for a list of jobs and instructions, as well as how to get back to the underground. To locate the Bandit cache:

  1. Go to the Oasis. The puzzle-maze has been disabled.
  2. Find the dead Loner and read his PDA. This solves the mystery of the spy, and you/Strelok send an SMS to Dimon.
  3. Go back to the puzzle-maze room and to the southeast corner.
  4. Turn north and jump up to the beam, and then to the air duct.
  5. Turn left (west) and follow the duct, low-crouching when necessary.
  6. Continue following the duct as it first turns right, and then turns left.
  7. The backpack is just past the left turn.

Plotnikov's first task, Find the three scientific suits, takes you to X-18. You can grab Syak's briefcase, too. Go to X-18, get the suits and briefcase. Then, go to Radar > Pripyat to drop off the briefcase and get the next task, Bring the new armor. On to Jupiter to give Plotnikov the suits. Continue doing Plotnikov's tasks until you get Find in X-8 briefcase with dosimeters. Now you have a bypass for the underground and you can return to Radar by going directly to Eastern Pripyat > Pripyat > Radar.

X-8∨

In Radar, go to the code-locked door accessed from the elevated platform at the end of the road to Pripyat. On your PDA, go to Diary > Journal > Door Codes to open it. Open the door on the right (west) and go down the steps to get a task update for the Find the X-25 laboratory in the Radar. Find and search the one corpse in a different uniform to find the prototype and get another update to see Kostya. Go on to Lab X-8, which is guarded by a Merc squad. Hop into the truck bed for cover and snipe them as they approach. When the way is clear, use the information from the ATP and X-10 flash drives, along with the Bolt's PDA information to open the lab door. Clear the lab of Le Havre's men and grab Plotnikov's dosimeters. If you've completed the Find the TOZ-34 and the Uzi task, locate the X-8 cache before you leave the lab. Return to Jupiter and take a look at your PDA's Diary Journal page for Information from Le Havre's Map to see the locations of two caches there. You can empty these caches as you finish Plotnikov's tasks. Next, go to the Eastern Pripyat bookstore and get that Le Havre cache. Now back to Army Warehouses to report finding the URT prototype.

URT∨

Your conversation with Kostya results in two more tasks, Speak with Sakharov and Find the cache in Pripyat. Go to Sakharov and he gives you the Find Vasiliev's PDA task in X-16. While you're in X-16 you can search for Matvei's tunnel cache. Return to Sakharov and give him the PDA. You should also have the key from Bring the teleporter to Cross. In turn, he returns the fully functional Handheld Teleportator (URT). Here's how it works:

  • Select a map from the left navigation. This loads the main window with locations within the selected map.
  • Select a location.
  • Select either a Battery or Moonlight artifact. Note that the artifact is destroyed.
  • Click Enter. It may lag a bit, but you see a white light and then the map loads with an autosave.

You do not get any locations when selecting a map that you don't have access to yet. Try transporting to Pripyat to look for Kostya's caches. When you give Kostya the PDA from Pripyat, he doesn't give you another task right away. Now you can look for Doc's medallion. If you have collected enough Anomolous Bisons for Cross, bring those along with you either before or after you get Doc's medallion.

Owl's Note∨

In the Kill Tusk and get his flashdrive task, Owl (one of Ghost's men) was killed and his flash drive contained information about a cache in X-10. Go through the lab until you get to the last room before the Brain Scorcher. In the northeast corner, jump on the ventilation equipment to find the backpack on top. Taking the note out of the backpack spawns a host of mutants, i.e., Controllers, Burers, and more Bloodsuckers. Once you're out of the lab, take the note to Ghost, and he gives you the Find the Engineering Plan task. Ghost sends you to Freeman, who sends you to Darkscape to find Tamaz and get some drawings.

Tamaz is located in the village west of the Stalkers. Because of the underbrush, you have to get in close. I suggest starting from the southeast corner of the complex. It doesn't take long for Tamaz's men to come at you. Go for headshots to conserve ammo and avoid getting caught reloading. When it's clear, find Tamaz and get the Detailed plan of the Radar bunker document. Take the document back to Freeman, who discovers a transition from the Brain Scorcher room to the Sarcophagus.

Unexplored Land∨
A few notes about this map: The map is full of Avengers. They like to sit along the paths ready to ambush. They also have the paths mined, so stay a few feet next to paths instead of traveling on them. You need to have your Firefighter Outfit from the Labyrinth to use the Fire Cave's Lower Entrance (this comes later on, so you don't need it right away).

When you spawn in Unexplored Land you are greeted by Astrologer. Finish the conversation and go north to find Hermit's camp. Hermit sends you to find a box of ammo that fell out of a helicopter. Go talk to one of the other Loners to get more information. Then follow the path north to the swamp. Along the way, look for Le Havre's cache. The path turns east and runs north of the swamp. Find the boulder along the north edge and go directly south to find the box along the south edge of the pool. Return with the box to complete the Find out about the box task. Then, go north of the camp to find the Old Man. Talk to him and then go into the cave and find the Gynecologist. He gives you the Learn about the medicines task. Since he doesn't want you to kill anyone, you need a Camouflaged Exoskeleton.

Use the URT to teleport to the Sarcophagus Wish Granter area, or use the X-10 teleport (which puts you on the opposite side of the area). While you are there, you can look for Charon's cache. You spawn in the northwest corner of the room, safe from Monolith for the moment. Remember this spot as there is a transition to open nearby later on. Lukash's cache is up near the roof and two of Charon's possible locations are on the way. The Camouflaged Exoskeleton suit has a weight limit of 100 kg, so you may want to stash anything you don't need for now so you can put the suit on as soon as you get it.

  1. Go south to the campfire and up the ramp.
  2. Go west to the top of the wall overlooking Wish Granter. Be aware that you are now visible to Monolith below.
  3. Turn north and jump up to each level until you are directly east of the large pillar (directly east of Wish Granter).
  4. Drop down on the east side to the ledge and go north to find a backpack in the corner. This is the first possible Charon cache location. If it's not there, go back to where you dropped down.
  5. Going west this time, either jump down to the ledge or low-crouch under the broken cement and drop down to the metal truss.
  6. Follow the ledge to the north side until you reach a ladder going up. If there is no backpack, continue on. This is the second possible Charon cache location.
  7. Climb the ladder and look south to see the truss going north and south.
  8. Make your way to the west side of the truss and move along to the south end. You may need to take out some Monolith to reduce the fire coming at you. But be aware that more are coming.
  9. At the south end, find the backpack between the south wall and the truss running east and west.
  10. Put the Camouflaged Exoskeleton on and make your way back down the ladder. Remember that the suit only keeps you invisible for short periods of time. You may need to find a safe spot to take it off and put it back on again.
  11. Go back to the first truss where there is an opening in the wall that you can use the lower part of the truss to get to.

If you did not find Charon's cache, see Charon for the remaining possibilities. If you dropped a backpack at the spawn point, retrieve it and use the URT to get to the Cordon. Now go to Cordon for the medicine. Use the URT to teleport to the west of the southern checkpoint. Go south, watching for mines, and jump over the fence. Go east to the square two-story building on the west side of the road and up the stairs. Collect the medicine and syringes, then go out the way you came in. Your next stop is the northern checkpoint to ask Akim about Miner's flashlights. Complete the Catch a pseudo dog puppy task for Akim to get the flashlights and take them to Miner to complete his tasks.

In Unexplored Land, give Gynecologist the medicine and talk with Jora about the safe. He gives you the Learn about the batteries task. Talk to the Old Man about the Fire Cave and then to Hermit about the location. The easiest way to find the Fire Cave is to go northwest to the western edge of the swamp that Hermit referenced. The entrance that you need is directly west, so you have to backtrack a little. Go about halfway down the mountain and look for a large boulder. The entrance is just to the south. If you have a bioradar, you will see someone there named Pilgrim. Talk to him and get the Find Pilgrim's Cache task. If you have the Camouflaged Exoskeleton suit, this is a simple fetch. You must pass an Avenger camp along the way. Go east along the path that runs south of the swamp to find the quarry. Approach the excavator from the southwest to jump on the tread. Work your way up to the west side of the cabin to find the backpack on the floor. Give Pilgrim the contents and he gives you Old Man's watch. You must come back for the batteries in 90 game-time minutes. Give the Old Man his watch to open transitions to Dark Valley and Army Warehouses.

Once you get the batteries from Pilgrim, give them to Hologram. It may not come right away, but shortly after completing the Learn about the batteries task you get an SMS from Hermit. Go talk to him and get the Learn about the Ultra Detectors task. Talk to Hologram and get the Learn about the Elite Detectors task. About this time you get an SMS from Adrenaline. Ignore it for now until you complete the Ultra Detectors task. Go to Sakharov to purchase two Elite detectors. He usually has only one, so you have to leave the map and come back for the second one (at 50,000 RUs each). Say to Sakharov, "Professor, I need to talk to you." and get the Find the group of scientists in laboratory X-10 and get all the details task. Bring the detectors and a Plasma Caterpillar artifact to Hologram and he sends you to Cyclops for a hint on the cache with the Ultra detectors.

Go back to Garbage and to the east end of the garbage pile south of the Flee Market. Find the backpack on the west side of the big dead tree. Take the detector to Adrenaline at the Agroprom factory and keep one for yourself. When you complete the task you get an SMS from Gynecologist that Cyclops has the safe open. So, back to Unexplored Land. Talk to Gynecologist to get the Learn about the floppy disk task. Talk to Cyclops for another hint, which opens a transition between the Unexplored Land and Labyrinth.

In Labyrinth, take the left of three passages, left at the first circular passage, and then three rights to get back to the small passage guarded by three Burner anomalies. Throw a bolt to activate the anomalies and go into the passage. Just ahead is a wounded Merc with the disc beside him. Take the disc and go back the way you came. Talk to Gynecologist to complete the task and get an SMS from an unknown stalker telling you to make a copy and give it to Astrologer. Then, talk to Cyclops about the flasks needed to make the medicine. See Reagent Flasks for instructions.

When you give Gynecologist the flasks, he tells you that he needs items from the Fire Cave and gives you the Find the power supply and the circuitboards task. He sends you north of the quarry to locate a key that will disable one of the teleports guarding the cave. Return to the eastern swamp and go northeast toward the small boulders on the map. Approach the anomaly field from the northwest toward two boulders together. On the opposite side (southeast) is a human skeleton and the teleport deactivator. Put on the Firefighter suit and take the key to the middle of the eastern swamp. The entrance to the cave is slightly southwest of the pond's center. You can see it if you low-crouch to get under the surface. Make your way through the passage taking care not to fall through the hole and meet up with Pilgrim again. He agrees to help you get the formula from the snipers and to let you search the cave for the other items.

Continue north to explore the cave. Notice that the passage closes behind you and you can no longer get to Pilgrim. At the first fork, go left and find a small room with two circuit boards and another teleport deactivator. Pilgrim doesn't seem to mind if you help yourself to the items in the dynamite box. Continue on south through the passage to find another small room with two power supplies (and again the passage closes behind you). These are all the items you need for Gynecologist. Continue on through the passage and turn left to exit the cave. Go southwest back to Gynecologist and report to complete the task. You get an SMS from Pilgrim that he messaged the Avengers. Go speak to Chimera in the trailer and get the Kill Sidorovich task (and for some strange reason, you also lose the teleport deactivators).

Report back to Gynecologist and get the Learn about solutions task, sending you to Owl (a different one), a veteran Hunter on the mountain between the eastern swamp and Hermit's camp. Gynecologist gives you five Chimera Claws and 30,000 RUs, but Owl is going to want 10 claws and 100,000 RUs. Hopefully, you've been stashing claws. When you talk to Owl he gives you the Learn about the controller corpse task that sends you to Radar. The Controller weighs 70 kgs. so be sure you can take on that much weight. Go to Radar, and to the hole in the fence near X-25. Stay north along the rocks, past the large tree to two large trees together. Find the Controller between the north tree and the rocks. Carry it back to Owl, who says, "Screw this, you're asking too much." but does the job anyway.

Tell Owl you have the payment and he tells you the head is over by the wood pile, which is at the north end of the camp. Take the head to Chimera, but he doesn't give you the formula. He does, however, tell you there's a cache, which is all Cyclops needs and he sends you an SMS. Chimera also offers a reward for finding a missing girl, but no task or information on how to go about finding her. Go back to Cyclops to get a hint on the location of the cache, which is in Radar. Go to the Radar complex and through the south opening on the west side. Use the ladder on the left to go up to the platform, then another short ladder to the next level. Use the broken duct to get to the roof of the L-shaped building along the south wall. Go east to the hole in the roof and use the going northwest to southeast to enter the building. Turn left (south) down the hallway. Find the backpack, doll, and flasks in the first room on the right (again, you can put the flasks in an artifact container). Take the flasks back to Gynecologist and he sends you to Cyclops for one more cache (a backpack on the ground next to the tractor in Bloodsucker Village). Oddly, Gynecologist only takes four of the six flasks. If you search the profuser, you can leave the last two there. You get an SMS from Fang telling you to meet him and Pilgrim at their new base, i.e., the Fire Cave. Put the Firefighter suit on and use the eastern swamp entrance. Again, no task appears on your PDA list.

Warlab∨

Your conversation with Fang opens transitions between Radar and Warlab, which is the same location as X-25. There is also a transition between Warlab and X-16, but you won't be able to access it yet. When you spawn in Warlab, there is a fight between Monolith and Mercenaries. My advice is to wait until the shooting stops, then go in and clear out any survivors. Find the Mercenary with a notebook and take it to Fang, who is now in Army Warehouses. If you're looting the dead and have too much to carry in one load, drop a backpack outside the Warlab and stash it all there, not inside the Warlab. Take all the stuff that Skinflint won't buy, like pistols and suits. Be sure to leave room for your camouflaged exoskeleton, and go meet Fang on the farmstead next to the Freedom Base. Fang sends you to Lukash to get a ring. But first, go to the Bar and sell your loot. Then, jump over to ATP for Charon's Blue flashes cache. Afterward, on to Lukash.

Lukash gives you the Find the ring task that takes you to the Labyrinth. Now go behind the HQ building and talk to Screw to get his task and go to the Wild Territory. Using the Wild Territory to Labyrinth transition puts you at the top level in Labyrinth. However, Spider is most likely on the second level. Starting from the Cave transition reduces the number of Monolith you have direct contact with. Either way, find Spider and get the ring. This starts an SMS exchange between you and Lukash. The end result is the Meeting in the Warlab task.

When you enter the Warlab, there are a few things to do in preparation for the next task:

  1. Go to down the stairs to the right (west) of the first guard.
  2. Go to the southeast corner and break out the wooden planks. Continue on and break the planks covering the ladder down.
  3. Go back out and across to the stairs going down to the room with the broken out floor.
  4. Turn left (south) through the doorway and follow the hallway into the room with a large platform.
  5. Go down the stairs and down the ladder in the southeast corner.
  6. Follow the passage to the last room on the left.
  7. Go to the end, going right around the anomaly. This triggers a Bloodsucker to spawn, but you're safe on the far side of the anomaly. If you're lucky, the Bloodsucker will run into the anomaly...problem solved.
  8. Now go back to the room with the broken out floor, drop down, and meet with Panther

Panther gives you the Retrieve the walkie talkie task. Give the walkie talkie to Panther and get the Get the stuffed psuedodog task from Dembel. Panther then gives you the Disk for Panther task, which is a priority. Enter the Radar complex from the south and find the lieutenant in, or near, the building set into the hillside. Take the disc back to Panther, get the Bring the suit task, and go on to Dark Valley (with your camouflaged exoskeleton). You can get Dembel's stuffed pseudodog and Charon's cache, too. Go to Army Warehouses > Unexplored Land > Dark Valley to get to the Bandit base. Get the suit for Panther, then go to Borov's office and grab the stuffed pseudodog on top of the cabinet in the southwest corner. Next, get Akim's PDA, Screw's vise, and on to Charon's Dark Valley cache.

Go back to the Warlab, give Dembel his trophy, and Panther her suit. Another Merc, Dushman, shows up and blocks our way out. No one seems to mind if you take out the Merc guard on the right (shoot through the glass). Otherwise, you'll have a tough time with this next part. Talk to Dushman, but only get close enough to be able to talk (too close and it doesn't go well), staying inside the room with Panther. Click through the conversation quickly (you can read it later) and jump to the left while Panther and her crew clear the lab. When the last Merc is down, talk to Panther and again to Dembel. Now take Lukash's ring back.

When you ask Lukash for your reward, he gives you another task instead, to escort Vitamin's group to the Warlab. At this point you get an SMS from Panther that she's leaving the Warlab. Talk to Vitamin and get the Help Freedom - Lead the group task. Meet Vitamin in Radar at the Army Warehouses transition, and lead the group to the fence opening to Warlab (they stop there). Talk to Vitamin again, and then move on to the Warlab. Inside, help Freedom clear out the pseudodogs. Vitamin turns off the teleport to the secret lab and stands in the same spot as Panther did. Talk to him to receive a Gravity Gun (very useful for getting artifacts out of anomalies). Now you can access the secret lab:

  1. Go south through the doorway and follow the passage left (east) to the next room with a catwalk around three sides.
  2. Drop down to the next level and go east through the doorway into the far elevator shaft.
  3. Climb up the ladder to the third level and into the secret lab.
  4. Find the ecologist, Lazy, in the orange suit and talk to him.
  5. Make your way up the stairs to the next room and talk to Klenov, who gives you the Speak with Victor Hologram task. You get a message from Fang and Cyclops, too.
Unfinished Business∨

The storyline is about to go in a direction involving new maps and characters. This is probably a good time to take care of some open tasks, such as:

  • Complete the order for weapons - You should be on the fifth order for three shotguns and an Igloment. The next two orders are for items you should have and can put in your backpack to save a trip.
  • Information for the fourth order - The two caches in Jupiter and Pripyat.
  • Cacheat the ChNPP-1
  • Bring information to Skinflint from the Deserter

You can:

  1. Get the Pripyat cache from Matvei's fourth order.
  2. Drop off his last two orders.
  3. Go on to the Jupiter cache from Matvei's fourth order.
  4. Go to the Jupiter Underground cache from Matvei's fifth order (use the Jupiter entrance).
  5. Get the Pripyat cache from Matvei's fifth order.
  6. Get Charon's ChNPP-1 cache.
  7. You can choose to go into the Sarcophagus for Matvei's last order cache, or wait and get it with Charon's X-16 cache.

Be aware that this task offers the Get the commanders orders in Agroprom, so you may want to pack your camouflaged exoskeleton. Lead him to the Army Warehouses transition. Continue on and take Skinflint his briefcase, and then meet Hologram in the Warlab. Bring along 10 Cobblestone artifacts and the Heart of a Poltergeist artifact if you have them. When you finish with Klenov, go to the Bar and meet Fang.

Red Forest∨

Fang and Pilgrim have relocated again to the Bar, more precisely, to the campfire directly west of Petrenko's location. Talk to Fang and get the Find Denis in the Red Forest and rescue him task. When you leave Rostok, go to Yantar and complete the Cachein Laboratory X-16 task. The Red Forest transition is along Radar's south cliffs near the east corner. I recommend you pack Ghost's rapid-fire Gauss, or whatever you prefer for dealing with Controllers and Bloodsuckers.

When you spawn in Red Forest you get a message and new task, Help the group of stalkers. Talk to Shadowman and agree to help them clear the Mercs off the bridge to Limansk to complete the task and get the Help stalkers capture the bridge in the Red Forest task. You can either stay with the group or move up the road and take the Mercs on yourself (and collect all the loot). Talk to Shadowman again when the bridge is clear to complete the task. Be sure to check the backpack in the truck bed on the Limansk side of the bridge. As a show of gratitude, Shadowman and his men leave you a small stash box of items in the middle of the road going south. If you have the Charon cache "Near the bridge", grab it now and move on to the mine.

Denis is in the Red Forest Mine, along with a couple Controllers, a Bloodsucker, civilian zombies, and kamikaze rats. You may want to first check on the stalker camp just outside the north gate to be sure there are no Mercs or Monolith. Then, find Denis and escort him through a band of Mercs to the Red Forest north gate. When you get Denis to the camp you send Fang an SMS. Denis couldn't find the documents, so Fang suggests you talk to Forester and gives you the Bring the documents from the Red Forest to Fang task. Complete this task and Fang sends you to the Rookie Village to meet with Astrologer. But first, get Charon's Cachein the Red Forest (if you haven't already) and cross the bridge into Limansk.

Limansk∨

Limansk is full of zombies, they seem to breed like rabbits. A shotgun with darts or slugs work the best (save the Gauss for mutants). Make your way through the city to meet Borman, a Freedom Master. He gives you the Help Borman task that has several steps to it. Continue on through the city and clear out the construction site. You get a task update when you get the last Monolith sniper. At some point you get an SMS to Meet with Professor Sakharov, which you can ignore for now. With the construction site clear, go outside to the west and find the radio jammer in an open shipping container. Take the jammer back to Skinflint at the Freedom Base in Army Warehouses. Skinflint needs five hours to work on the jammer. Now go meet Astrologer in the Rookie Village.

Astrologer asks for Fang's map and then asks you to kill Panther. Tell him you'll think about it and that you must see Sidorovich about something (which is just an excuse to leave). You then get an SMS from Fang that Pilgrim is missing and the Pripyat to Old Village transition opens. But before you meet Pilgrim you need to finish in Limansk. So, back to Army Warehouses to pick up the jammer, and then back to Limansk. Put the radio jammer back where you found it (you must drop it from your backpack: you don't get a prompt).

Continue north through the construction site to the Hospital transition. In the hospital, talk to Cat and lead the Loners through a gauntlet of Monolith and Bloodsuckers. When you drop through the hole at the end of the second section, the Find the Black Doctor task is completed and you talk to the Black Doctor. At the end of the rather lengthy conversation, you pass out for a couple of minutes. When you wake up, continue down the stairs and through the doorway. You are then transported to the first section and can talk to Cat if you want (it's optional). Then leave the hospital and report to Borman that the jammer is in place to complete the Help Borman task. Now go from Limansk to Red Forest > Radar > Yantar. Complete the Meet with Professor Sakharov task. Go to the Cachein the Wild Territory, back to Limansk for that cache, and on to Old Village.

Old Village∨

When you spawn in Old Village you find yourself in a trap with Pilgrim and one other stalker. Talk to Pilgrim and learn more on the situation. Try to walk through the teleport and you get sent back. Another stalker, Kolmogor, disables the teleport and comes in. Talk to him to get the Help Kolmogor task. This leads to the Help capture the headquarters task. As you complete the task and enter the house, you get an SMS from Panther telling you she wants to meet, but no time or location. Talk to Kolmogor and get the Survey the village task. Upon completing the task, Panther sends another SMS telling you she's in the Swamp.

Complete the Find out where Panther is and Protect the village tasks, talk to Pilgrim about him staying with Panther, and get the Speak with Klenov task. Pick up the Cachein Old Village and head back to Pripyat > Radar > Warlab. At some point, probably near or in Warlab, you get a series of SMS messages about Panther running off.

At this point, it is very difficult to tie the Charon Caches with the storyline. There is nothing in the remaining caches that you probably don't already have as far as weapons, ammo, artifacts, and suits. If you're partial to the Monolith Exoskeleton, you may want to complete the last few cache tasks now.
Generators∨

In Warlab, talk to Klenov and he gives you the Find the psi-antenna task. Complete the task and leave Warlab. As you do, Panther sends you an SMS explaining why she ran away. You send Pilgrim a message telling him you're on your way to him. You must complete all Ghost tasks before going any further. Check with him in Radar before you go any further.

Go to Old Village and talk to Pilgrim. This starts a series of SMS messages ending with Ghost offering to meet you at CNPP1. At CNPP1, get inside the gate and go west to the road. Follow the road west until it turns northwest and go between the building on the left (south) and a short cement wall on the right (north). You must go under a series of large pipes. You then come up to a Military officer, which you should take out and get Sakharov's bag from him. Be careful of the nearby BTR. Continue west to the next set of pipes. After you pass under the pipes and reach the end of the short wall on the right (north), turn northwest and find Ghost in the northwest corner of the area. Talk to him and he leads you back the way you came to a gate just east of where you encountered the Military officer and BTR. Ghost stops here for you to talk again. Then, proceed to the transition.

In Generators, talk with Ghost and he leads you to the Loner camp. In the camp, approach the guarded doorway and get an SMS that you must talk with Countryman. He gives you a choice of paying for information or the Shoot down the helicopter task. Take your Gauss to the road at the north part of the map (it takes less than two minutes to get there), and find the helicopter high overhead. When the chopper crashes, go back to Countryman and report. He tells you of a scientist, Andersen, that escaped the base and is being guarded by Fritz. Go talk to Anderson. As you do, another helicopter arrives carrying Black Doctor. Talk to Black Doctor, then to Ghost, then back to Black Doctor to get a ride to the Swamps and the Find Boatswain task. Walk up to the helicopter behind the landing gear to get on board.

Back at the Generators, talk to Boatswain and then to Andersen to get the Bring the gamepad task. Using the map in the Detailed Task Information, go to location 5 and climb up from the northwest using the tree branch/root and find the gamepad on top. Take it back to the camp, talk to Andersen, and then to Boatswain to get the Disable the Psi-antennas task. Take Boatswain to the base as far as possible until a counter starts and you have eight seconds to get back out of the danger zone. Talk to Boatswain again and wait for him to disable the antennas, then return. Sometimes Boatswain gets stuck in the base, so be sure to watch your map and save when he gets close to the base. When he returns, go back to camp and talk to Ghost, then wait for SMSs from Fritz and Black Doctor's associate, Sapsan. Talk to Ghost again to get the Save Panther task and start for the base's front gate.

After clearing the base, you get a message to check the tunnel between the fire pit and guard tower. You may encounter a couple more Monoliths down there. When you reach the other end of the tunnel, you see Astrologer getting into a helicopter and Panther lies dead. A series of SMSs comes in and Black Doctor heads your way. When he gets there, talk to him and then to Ghost. Head back to camp and talk to Boatswain, then again to Ghost to get Panther's Notebook with the code to open the Generators to Warlab door. This prompts another series of SMSs with Black Doctor ending with the Get the dossier task.

First, go to the base and open the coded door. This opens into the stairs leading from Radar to the Warlab. So, you go up the stairs to Radar and down the stairs to Warlab. Anyway, once you open the door, go back to the Generators and complete the dossier task. With the dossier, open your inventory, put the dossier in your pistol/weapon 2 slot, close your inventory, and activate the dossier. It's in Russian, but you really don't need to read it as there is more SMSs and you go to Unexplored Land (and bring your Firefighter suit) with the Find the cave task.

Saving Fly∨

In Unexplored Land, there is a plateau in the far northwest corner with large boulders. You can see someone on your minimap and bioradar. Low-crouch under the boulders to find the cave opening and complete the Find the cave task. You message Fang and he replies that you should come to the Fire Cave before trying to enter. Put your Firefighter suit on and head for the Fire Cave. Talk to Fang and then lead him back to the secret cave. To do this, you must stay in the valley and go north, and then west as Fang will not follow you up the steep incline.

At the cave, Fang disables the teleport protecting the entrance. Go down two ladders to the girl, Fly, and talk to her. Fang sends a message that someone is coming, so leave the cave and wait with him for the helicopter to land. When Panther and Astrologer get out go talk to Panther, and then back to Fang. Lead Fly to Hermit's camp. Along the way you exchange messages with Panther, and when you enter the camp you get a message from Gynecologist. Go talk to him and get the Get the artifacts from Klenov task. Talk to Fly and get the Find an old doll task, talk to Fang, and then head for Radar. As you travel, you will need a Ruby Spring artifact in the very near future, which you should have in your cache. Get the artifacts from Klenov and Fly's doll, then return to Hermit's camp. Give Fly her doll, talk to Fang, and head back to Rostok and talk with Voronin.

Voronin agrees to help, but needs a few minutes before he sends a message about the helicopter crashing in CNPP2. This gives you the Find the flight recorder task. You have a few options for getting to CNPP2. The shortest way without using the URT is Rostok > Army Warehouses > Radar > X16 > Sarcophagus > CNPP2. Dead City∨
The teleport to Dead City has a security system that prohibits any weapons except a knife or sword, 9x19 and 9x18 pistols with magazine capacities of 13 or less, sawn-off shotguns, and what ever suit you're wearing. You will have a chance to pick up weapons that use 9x19mm, 5.45x39mm, and 5.56x45mm ammo, along with Gauss batteries. You can also take under-barrel grenade launchers. The Gravity Gun is also prohibited. There's a safe to store the prohibited items and it's recommended that you bring your favorite weapons to store there. Also, bring an anomalous M-16 and the camouflaged exoskeleton suit.

In Limansk, go to the north side of the Gastronom building and push your way through the door. The next door is slightly open and you can shoot the box blocking (or use a knife).   • Bring the laptop to Arhara's brother - Find the backpack on the ground east of the helicopter about halfway to the boulders. When you take the items, cloning pseudodogs spawn. Cat tasks∨

Cat is a sick man and needs help with the following tasks:

  • Search the corpse of a Monolith and find a map for Cat - Follow Cat to the Burnt Farmstead. Along the way, there's an Avenger waiting to ambush you. And if you haven't killed the electrified bloodsucker yet, you'll need to do that now. Then, loot the corpse and talk to Cat. He goes back to the Clear Sky base to rest. The next time you're at the base, talk to him about the next task.
  • Find the remaining Monoliths, Cat needs their personal notebooks - The first part of this task doesn't show on your PDA. You need to find someone on the Clear Sky base that can help find the Monoliths. Go into the lab (Professor Kalancha's lab) and find Kostik Sharptoothed. As with all Zone characters, Kostik wants something in exchange, a television. Just like the one next to Akim in Cordon. Bring the television to Kostik and he gives you possible locations of the remaining two Monoliths on your map. Check each location, kill the Monoliths, and bring the notebook back to Cat. Cat needs more time to study the notes and suggests meeting at the Bar.
  • Get Kalmyk's medicine for Cat - Meet up with Cat again at the Bar and he gives you this task. This is a different type of medicine than what you got for Vasily. See Kalmyk for instructions.
  • Get information of the Monolith in the Agroprom for Cat - South of the Agroprom Institute building (where you met Arhara), find the Monolith and two Loners. Kill the Monolith and talk to the Loners. You may encounter a bug here where the Loners pick up weapons from the fallen Monolith and attack the Military in the institute complex. There doesn't seem to be any other option than to kill everything in the area so that you can talk to the Loners. If that happens, you can grab Lukash's dynamite on the second floor. When you can talk to the Loners, they ask you to guide them to the factory complex. Once they are safe, talk to them again to get more information. Take the information back to Cat and he gives you the Find the trader Rabinovich task.
  • Find the trader Rabinovich - In Pripyat, go through the underground car park and north up the road on the east side of the hotel. Just past the hotel and before you get to the Ferris Wheel, you can see a neutral stalker in the building on the east side. Use the rubble to climb up and into the open window. In the next room is Rabinovich. Talk to him until he gives you the information you're after, then he collapses. Eventually, he becomes hostile and you have to put him down. You get a task update to return to Cat, and a new task Find the Black Doctor. Back at the Bar, Cat suggest talking to Zahar to find a way to Limansk, the Black Doctor's location.
  • Find the Black Doctor - Make your way through Limansk to the Hospital. Talk to Cat and lead him through the first two sections. The first section is guarded by Monolith. There are two options for advancing into the second section:
    • The upper level has a hole in the wall in the southeast corner.
    • The lower level has a doorway in the south wall.

Vasily is the Clear Sky repair technician, and a member of the Traders. He is also Sidorovich's brother and you/Marked One mistake him for Sid. Go to what was Novikov's hut and ask him what he is doing in the Swamp. He gives you the Get 3 cans of Kalmyk Ointment for Vasily task. Go to the Pump Station, look inside the backpack on the floor, and take the grenade. This is your introduction to Kalmyk, who gives you the Obtain for Kalmyk his container task. The container is west of the Pump Station near the west end of an island (just east of the tall grass). The trick to this task is to find the right direction to approach the container without getting transported back to the east end of the island. You need to systematically approach the container from a random direction, and moving either left or right about 10 steps (or points of the compass) and trying it again. Keep moving in the same direction until you find the window in the anomaly.

Before returning to Kalmyk, continue north to the area with the controller. See Bring the unusual controller brain to Sviblov. Once you have the brain, head back to the Pump Station. Give Kalmyk the container and buy three bottles of ointment (you have to ask three times) using either cigarettes or vodka. Now head back to Clear Sky's base.

You may be able to pick up an AEK-971 from the attackers. This weapon is required for another task.

Zahar tasks∨

When you complete the Bring the disc to Adrenaline task you get the Speak to Zahar task.

  • Protect the hunters at the ATP - I recommend a good sniper rifle and plenty of daylight for this task. Go to the cabin from Bring the disc to Adrenaline and meet the Hunter leader, Sepator. The conversation is interrupted by the Bandits. Sometimes, the Bandits all spawn in one place north of the cabin and you can take most of them out with a grenade launcher. Other times they come at you from north, east, and west.In that case, use the tractor for cover and go east to west with your sniper rifle. When it's clear, talk to Sepator again, take the backpack out of the cabin, and turn it over to Zahar.
  • Destroy the Merc camp at Rostok - There is a group of Mercs just past the transition who attack any Dutiers. Take the Mercs out and report back to Zahar.
  • Find Brother Louis in the Swamps - Go to the Swamps and use the same approach as with Kill Tusk and get his flashdrive. Talk to Brother Louis and learn the Frenchman is hiding on the Freedom Base. As you walk away, you hear a gun shot. Go back and find Brother Louis dead. In Army Warehouses, go to the Freedom Base HQ building and up to the southeast room to find a dead Frenchman. Take out the nearby Avengers and turn the Frenchman's PDA over to Zahar.
    It's impossible to save the Frenchman as the assassins don't spawn until he's already dead.
